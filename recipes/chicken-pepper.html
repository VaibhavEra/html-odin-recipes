Ingredients

  • Pepperfry masala
  • BLACK PEPPERCORNS
  • CUMIN SEEDS
  • FENNEL SEEDS
  • CURRY LEAVES
  • CHICKEN
  • GINGER GARLIC GREEN CHILLI PASTE
  • TURMERIC POWDER

Steps

  1. Set a small pan over high heat & once it gets hot, lower the flame & add fennel seeds, cumin seeds & black peppercorns
  2. keep stirring & dry roast them over low flame until the cumin seeds turn light brown, then transfer them into a bowl, cool them down completely & grind into a fine powder.
  3. Set a kadhai over high heat, add oil & let it get hot.
  4. Add cumin & fennel seeds, garlic & curry leaves, stir well & cook briefly.Then add the onions, stir & cook over high heat until the onions turn light golden brown, then add the ginger garlic & green chilli paste, stir & cook for 1-2 minutes.Further lower the flame & add turmeric powder & garam masala, stir & add the chicken along with salt.
  5. Stir & cook the chicken over high flame for 4-5 minutes, then add a splash of water, cover & cook over low flame until the chicken is cooked.Once the chicken is cooked, remove the lid & add the pepper fry masala along with curry leaves & green chilli & cook over high flame while stirring gently until all the moisture gets evaporated.
  6. Lastly taste & adjust salt if required & add lemon juice & fresh coriander, stir well.
  7. Chicken pepper fry is ready, serve it with malabar parotta.
